- Meta-ethics (moral realism, relativism, non-cognitivism)
- Normative ethics
  - Virtue ethics (Aristotle, MacIntyre)
  - Deontological ethics (Kant, Ross)
  - Consequentialism and utilitarianism (Bentham, Mill, Singer)
- Applied ethics (bioethics, environmental ethics, political philosophy)
- Social contract theory (Hobbes, Locke, Rousseau, Rawls)
